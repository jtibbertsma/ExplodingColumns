Exploding Columns

Controls

  • a: move left
  • d: move right
  • w: rotate
  • s: drop
  • p: pause

Instructions

  • Connect four tiles of the same color to make them explode!
  • Watch out for avalanches!
  • Your tiles will begin to fall faster after a while.
  • You can slow your tiles back down by getting a combo.
  • The game lasts until the middle column fills up.
  • Survive for as long as possible!
